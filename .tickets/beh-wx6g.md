# Living Places Benchmark v1

Turn the accepted San Francisco and Lower Manhattan artifacts into measured living places. Benchmark correspondence, legibility, habitability, native Minecraft ecology, experiential quality, and runtime capacity without coupling to Behold controllers.

## Design

Minecraft remains authoritative. Accepted worlds are immutable fixtures; benchmark runtimes are disposable clones. Evidence is profile- and place-specific. Results publish a score vector and Pareto recommendations, not one synthetic quality score.

## Acceptance Criteria

Both accepted places run through the same reproducible harness; structural, ground/aerial, ecology, and performance evidence is packaged; regional defects and smart defaults are published; full repository gate passes; no Behold controller dependency is introduced.
