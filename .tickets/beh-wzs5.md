# Living City Foundry v2

Turn the two-city Living Places proof into a self-improving multi-place foundry: reusable quality repair, a third structurally distinct place, real human experience, accelerated wide/populated simulation, and the narrow Behold-owned epoch junction, with deletion-oriented canonicalization and independently verified release evidence.

## Design

Place owns recipes, immutable artifacts, runtime profiles, geographic/presentation evidence, and disposable benchmark runtimes. Behold owns package ingestion, world epochs, leases, bodies, minds, memory, actions, and consequence witnesses. Minecraft remains authoritative. Progress is a production event stream; green claims require packaged evidence.

## Acceptance Criteria

SF and Manhattan measurably improve without weakening v1; a third place is accepted without compiler-specific code; route/sightline/tour, survival, accelerated-time, active-region, controlled entity/population, and resource evidence are honest and packaged; packaged place launches through a Behold-owned epoch adapter; superseded duplication/cruft is removed or versioned; deterministic release, full gates, closed children, clean worktrees.
