population proof with the normal direct mind, a runner-owned OpenRouter
credential, and aggregate model concurrency capped at one.

separate Lync autobiographies contained five and six prior turns. Each body

## What the failed runs taught us

The path to green mattered because each red result belonged to a different
boundary:

1. Optional tool calls let a model answer in prose. Resident decisions now
   require one explicit action while leaving the particular action to the
   model.
2. The items were outside the first-person view. Restoring the ordinary ability
   to look fixed perception without granting an omnidirectional item sensor.
3. The verifier still equated free choice with the old `auto` wire value. The
   shared rule now accepts `auto` or `required` only when the chosen tool was
   genuinely offered beside at least one alternative; exact-function and
   single-choice forcing remain failures.
4. Repeated historical cursor scaffolding pushed an otherwise causal run 681
   tokens over budget. Historical working context is now a compact causal delta
   over the unchanged append-only evidence, reducing the exact restart bodies
   by 17–19 percent.
5. A one-axis look command made residents spend separate actions on horizontal
   and vertical orientation. The action now exposes the two orthogonal degrees
   of freedom in one ordinary glance.
6. Luna still oscillated and exhausted its eight-step episode. On the exact
   failed request, Gemini used both axes in 3/3 matched trials; Haiku did so in
   0/3. Gemini then earned the default by passing this full live proof.

These are examples of the intended feedback loop: locate whether a failure is
in observation, affordance, cognition, scheduling, verification, or budget;
change only the owning boundary; rerun the whole lived story.

## What this does not prove

This is not a city, household, or sustained society benchmark. It proves two
residents under one aggregate cognition cap, not three or hundreds. The task
and two loose resources were staged. It does not establish open-ended survival,
crafting, architecture, communication, contention, local-model scheduling,
multi-hour cost stability, or visual understanding of culturally complex
builds. The next population rung should vary inhabitants and active regions in
a continuing ordinary world, rather than multiply more pickup scenarios.
